Epson setup instructions
  1. Connect laptop to Epson via HDMI.
  2. Click Epson 3D preset above (sets SBS + fullscreen).
  3. On Epson remote: 2D/3D button → 3D Format: Side-by-Side.
  4. Pair your active shutter glasses (per projector manual).
  5. Press F here or Epson's 3D Inversion button if eyes feel swapped.
